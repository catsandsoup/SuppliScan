# App Review Notes Draft

Use this as the App Store Connect Review Notes field after final OCR verification.

No account is required.

SuppliScan is an iPhone-only app for scanning supplement labels and generating reviewable reference reports. Camera access is used to capture supplement-label images for on-device text recognition. The app can also import an existing label photo. Scan history is stored locally on device.

Suggested review flow:
1. Launch the app.
2. Tap `Scan a label`.
3. Capture or import a supplement facts label.
4. Review the extracted rows.
5. Tap `Analyse` to generate the report.
6. Open `History` to verify the report is saved locally.

Clinical boundary:
SuppliScan is a reference and documentation tool. Reports include a disclaimer and do not replace advice from a qualified healthcare professional.

Known launch blocker:
Do not submit this text until the OCR overhaul is complete and the final OCR flow has been tested with real labels.
